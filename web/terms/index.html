Description of Service

Vaultaire is an on-device encrypted photo and file vault for iOS. The App encrypts your data locally using a pattern-based key derivation system. We do not operate servers that store your data (aside from optional iCloud backup to your personal Apple account and optional vault sharing via Apple CloudKit).

Your Responsibilities

  • Remember your pattern. Your encryption key is derived from your pattern. If you forget your pattern and have not saved your recovery phrase, your data cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.
  • Save your recovery phrase. During setup, you are given a recovery phrase. Store it securely. It is the only way to recover your vault if you forget your pattern.
  • Lawful use. You agree to use the App only for lawful purposes and in accordance with all applicable laws.
  • Content responsibility. You are solely responsible for the content you store in the App.

No Data Recovery

Vaultaire uses zero-knowledge encryption. We do not have access to your encryption keys, patterns, or recovery phrases. If you lose your pattern and recovery phrase, your data is permanently unrecoverable. We cannot assist with data recovery under any circumstances.
